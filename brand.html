Get to Know Us!

It started out as an interest of mine, then transitioned into a hobby. There was a time that I wanted it to be self-sustaining, so I figured I should sell these custom press-ons that are good quality, but at a lower price for students like me. Considering that I am surrounded by creatives I figured that people will be interested. From then on, I got more and more orders and got offers to work in a few photoshoots with professional models until pan.de.kuko became what it is today.

Vision & Mission

To be one of the leading press-on nail brand for creatives and fashion enthusiasts who value unique, high-fashion nail art.

To be a trusted brand that consistently delivers innovative designs, exceptional quality, and inspiration for self-expression.
